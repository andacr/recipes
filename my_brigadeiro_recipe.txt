Brazillian Brigadeiro Recipe

1 can of sweet condensed milk
1 tablespoon of butter without salt
4 tablespoon of powder chocolate
1/2 can of milk cream

How to make: Mix all the ingredients in a pan until it get homogenous, leaving behind only the milk cream. Turn on the fir and stir it until the brigadeiro begins to unstick from the bottom of the pan, then turn off the fire and add the milk cream, mix it until it gets homogenous, and it's done.
